                           ROSARY MYSTERIES - APPLICATION DOCUMENTATION
================================================================================

OVERVIEW
--------
Rosary Mysteries is an Android application designed to help Catholics pray the
Rosary. It displays the 20 mysteries of the Rosary organized into four sets,
with each mystery linking to the corresponding Bible passage on YouVersion.

Package: com.rosary.mysteries
Version: 1.0.1 (versionCode 2)
Min SDK: 26 (Android 8.0)
Target SDK: 35


================================================================================
                                    FEATURES
================================================================================

MYSTERIES
---------
The app contains all 20 mysteries of the Rosary:

  JOYFUL MYSTERIES (Monday, Saturday)
    1. The Annunciation - Lk 1:26-38 - Fruit: Humility
    2. The Visitation - Lk 1:39-56 - Fruit: Love of neighbor
    3. The Nativity - Lk 2:1-20 - Fruit: Poverty, detachment
    4. The Presentation - Lk 2:22-40 - Fruit: Obedience
    5. Finding in the Temple - Lk 2:41-52 - Fruit: Seeking God in all things

  SORROWFUL MYSTERIES (Tuesday, Friday)
    1. The Agony in the Garden - Mt 26:36-46 - Fruit: Contrition for sins
    2. The Scourging at the Pillar - Mt 27:26 - Fruit: Mortification of the senses
    3. The Crowning with Thorns - Mt 27:27-31 - Fruit: Contempt of the world
    4. The Carrying of the Cross - Mk 15:21-22 - Fruit: Patience in trials
    5. The Crucifixion - Lk 23:33-46 - Fruit: Forgiveness of injuries

  GLORIOUS MYSTERIES (Wednesday, Sunday)
    1. The Resurrection - Lk 24:1-12 - Fruit: Faith
    2. The Ascension - Mk 16:19-20 - Fruit: Hope and desire for Heaven
    3. The Descent of the Holy Spirit - Acts 2:1-13 - Fruit: Gifts of the Holy Spirit
    4. The Assumption - Lk 1:46-55 - Fruit: Grace of a happy death
    5. The Coronation - Rev 12:1-6 - Fruit: Devotion to Mary

  LUMINOUS MYSTERIES (Thursday)
    1. The Baptism in the Jordan - Mt 3:13-17 - Fruit: Fidelity to baptismal promises
    2. The Wedding at Cana - Jn 2:1-12 - Fruit: Trust in Mary
    3. The Proclamation of the Kingdom - Mk 1:14-15 - Fruit: Conversion of heart
    4. The Transfiguration - Mt 17:1-9 - Fruit: Desire for holiness
    5. The Institution of the Eucharist - Mt 26:26-30 - Fruit: Love of the Eucharist

BIBLE DEEP LINKS
----------------
Tapping a mystery opens the corresponding Bible passage in YouVersion (bible.com).
The app selects the appropriate Bible translation based on the device language:
  - English: ESV (version 59)
  - French: Louis Segond 1910 (version 93)
  - Spanish: Reina Valera 1960 (version 149)
  - Italian: Nuova Riveduta 2006 (version 122)

AUTOMATIC MYSTERY SELECTION
---------------------------
The app automatically displays today's mysteries based on the traditional schedule:
  - Monday, Saturday: Joyful
  - Tuesday, Friday: Sorrowful
  - Wednesday, Sunday: Glorious
  - Thursday: Luminous

Users can manually select any mystery set via the navigation drawer.

HOW TO PRAY GUIDE
-----------------
The app includes a guide explaining how to pray the Rosary:
  - Introduction explaining the four mystery sets
  - Beginning prayers
  - How to pray each decade
  - Closing prayers

================================================================================
                                  DESIGN STYLE
================================================================================

AESTHETIC DIRECTION
-------------------
"Illuminated Manuscript" - inspired by medieval religious manuscripts with warm,
elegant colors evoking parchment, gold leaf, and aged leather.

TYPOGRAPHY
----------
Two Google Fonts are used:

  LORA (Serif) - For titles and headings
    - Weights: Regular, Medium, SemiBold, Bold, Italic
    - Used in: Display, Headline, Title styles
    - Character: Elegant, slightly calligraphic serifs

  CRIMSON TEXT (Serif) - For body text and labels
    - Weights: Regular, SemiBold, Bold, Italic
    - Used in: Body, Label styles
    - Character: Classical, highly readable

COLOR PALETTE
-------------

  LIGHT THEME (Parchment aesthetic):
    - Background: Parchment (#FDF8F3) - warm off-white like aged paper
    - Surface: Cream (#FFFDF9) - slightly lighter
    - Primary: Sepia (#2C1810) - deep brown-black for text
    - Secondary: Gold Leaf (#D4A84B) - rich gold for accents
    - Tertiary: Umber (#6B5344) - muted brown
    - Surface Variant: Vellum (#F0E6D8) - warmer beige

  DARK THEME (Candlelit reading aesthetic):
    - Background: Obsidian (#0F0D0B) - deep warm black
    - Surface: Dark Leather (#1C1714) - like old book binding
    - Primary: Antique Cream (#E5D9C9) - warm text color
    - Secondary: Candlelight (#EAC87C) - glowing gold
    - Tertiary: Warm Amber (#CFB896) - muted gold
    - Surface Variant: Mahogany (#3D2E26) - rich wood tone

ANIMATIONS & INTERACTIONS
-------------------------
  - Staggered fade-in + slide-up for mystery cards on load
  - Animated title transitions when switching mystery sets
  - Touch feedback: scale animation (97%) on mystery card press
  - Spring-based animations for smooth, natural feel

LAYOUT
------
  - Modal navigation drawer for mystery selection
  - Clean vertical list of mystery cards
  - Each card shows: number badge, title, Bible reference, description, fruit
  - Subtle dividers between mysteries
  - Edge-to-edge design with transparent status/navigation bars

ICONS
-----
Lucide icon set (com.composables:icons-lucide):
  - Menu (hamburger)
  - ArrowLeft (back navigation)
  - BookOpen (How to Pray)
  - Settings (gear)
  - Heart (Support)
  - Check (selection indicator)

                              TECHNICAL ARCHITECTURE

STACK
-----
  - Language: Kotlin
  - UI Framework: Jetpack Compose + Material 3
  - Navigation: Compose Navigation
  - Persistence: DataStore Preferences
  - Build: Gradle 9.2.1 + AGP 8.7.x

PROJECT STRUCTURE
-----------------
  com.rosary.mysteries/
    ├── MainActivity.kt          - Entry point, theme application
    ├── domain/
    │   ├── Mystery.kt           - Mystery data class
    │   ├── MysterySet.kt        - Mystery set (type + mysteries)
    │   └── MysteryType.kt       - Enum with day-based selection
    ├── data/
    │   ├── MysteriesRepository.kt   - All mystery data
    │   ├── ThemePreferences.kt      - Theme persistence
    │   └── PreferencesManager.kt    - Language preferences
    └── ui/
        ├── Navigation.kt        - NavHost setup
        ├── theme/
        │   └── Theme.kt         - Colors, typography, theme
        ├── screens/
        │   ├── HomeScreen.kt    - Main screen with drawer
        │   ├── SettingsScreen.kt
        │   └── HowToScreen.kt
        └── components/
            └── MysteryCard.kt   - Individual mystery display

LOCALIZATION
------------
Fully translated in 4 languages:
  - res/values/strings.xml (English - default)
  - res/values-fr/strings.xml (French)
  - res/values-es/strings.xml (Spanish)
  - res/values-it/strings.xml (Italian)

Language can be changed in-app via Settings, using:
  - LocaleManager (Android 13+)
  - AppCompatDelegate (older versions)
